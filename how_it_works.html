How Enchanted Auctions Works

Once you've found a relic that calls to you, place your bid with confidence. Our transparent bidding system allows you to track auction progress in real-time.

We offer a comprehensive magical authenticity guarantee. If the relic's magical properties do not meet the description, we provide a full refund within 30 days of acquisition.
