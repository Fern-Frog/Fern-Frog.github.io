About these lands

"Acharia is a realm where the boundaries between the natural world and the arcane are almost nonexistent. The region is dominated by towering forests that are as old as the world itself, their roots entwined with the history of Loreveil. Ancient myths speak of spirits that inhabit the trees, guarding the secrets of the forest and watching over travelers who respect its rules. The people of Acharia live in harmony with the land, crafting their homes from living trees and learning to communicate with the creatures of the wild.
The magic of Acharia is deeply rooted in nature and emotion. Druids and healers from this region are known for their ability to channel the life force of the forest into powerful spells and remedies. Seasonal celebrations mark the passage of time, with festivals held under moonlit glades, accompanied by the haunting music of natural instruments. Though Acharia is a place of great beauty, it is also one of great caution; the woods are home to elusive creatures, both wondrous and dangerous, and it is said that those who wander too far without guidance may never return.
Despite its isolation, Acharia has always played a significant role in the balance of magic across Loreveil, providing wisdom and stability to a world in constant flux. It is a land that asks for respect but offers immeasurable rewards to those who seek to understand its rhythms."
